on the Parasympathetic side, the functions say: constricts pupil, stimulates salivation, slows heart rate, constricts bronchi, stimulates digestion, stimulates bile scretion, causes bladder to contract. On the Sympathetic side, the functions say: dilates pupil, inhibits salivation, increases heart rate, dilates bronchi, inhibits digestion near the solar plexus, stimulates the breakdown of glycogen, stimulates secretion of adrenaline and noradrenaline, inhibits contraction of bladder
